Our History

Founded in 1895, Oblivion University began as a small liberal arts college with just 50 students. Over the past century, we've grown into a comprehensive university serving over 20,000 students across 120 degree programs.

Our Mission

Oblivion University is committed to providing accessible, high-quality education that prepares students for meaningful careers and engaged citizenship. We foster intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and lifelong learning.
